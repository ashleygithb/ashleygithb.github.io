Core Values

Values are fundamental to us. Knowing ourselves and preparing for life is also fundamental.

IT

Application prototyping, Microsoft tools, programming and much more prepare us for the new technological era.

To develop young people with potential and desire for self-improvement, so that through comprehensive training they can opt for better opportunities for professional training and work, to improve their quality of life and their environment.

To be a program of academic excellence that contributes to raise the educational level of the youth of Central America, in order to provide them with new and better life opportunities through high quality training in English, computer skills and values.

The base values of the program are: Overcoming, excellence, solidarity, integrity and leadership

Our calls to action are: Walk the extra mile, Raise the bar and Giving back.
About Us

The ¡Supérate! program and its impact

The ¡Supérate! program is a high-performance scholarship that opens the doors of self-improvement to countless young people! Exploring the world of English, values and technology, it gives them the opportunity to shine. In addition, we enrich their journey with exciting activities that bring out their skills and talents.
